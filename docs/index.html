Why Size Tiers Matter

Comparing an 8B model to a 405B model is like comparing a sports car to a freight truck. Three tiers let you find the fastest or best model within your hardware budget:

Small ≤10B

100–300+ tok/s · edge & mobile friendly · minimal VRAM

Medium 10–50B

30–100 tok/s · balanced performance · 1–2 GPU setup

Large 50B+

5–40 tok/s · maximum quality · multi-GPU or API only
